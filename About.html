Who We Are

DonateCare is a non-profit initiative created to connect donors with people who need food and clothing the most.

What We Do

We collect surplus food and wearable clothes and distribute them safely to shelters, orphanages, and low-income families.

Why It Matters

Millions suffer from hunger and lack of clothing while resources go to waste. Your donation helps change that.
